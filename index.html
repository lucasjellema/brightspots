Bright Spots Survey Overview

Insights from the breadth of the Conclusion ecosystem into new demand from customers: what do we notice interest in, what new challenges, technology, concepts, suppliers are our customers working on or want to work with? And what developments do we ourselves see? With the information we gather this way, we hope to get a picture of new and evolving demand. We can then see whether our portfolio can handle that demand and, if not, whether we see the potential (and maybe even inevitability) of the demand and then start defining an action to do something for it. That could be a new proposition from one or more companies and also possibly trigger M&A activity.
